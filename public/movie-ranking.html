The Best Albums According to Jacob

My top 3 Albums of all-time.

The Strokes' Is This It

Such a unique approach to rock/pop songwriting and has a lot of style.

Hiatus Kaiyote's Choose Your Weapon

Funky rhythms and grooves.

Bill Evans Trio's Waltz for Debby

Beautifully melancholy and nostalgic tone. A master class in group improvisational playing
